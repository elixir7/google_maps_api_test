Instructions

This is a demo for the Karmy team.

To test the demo follow these instructions.

  1. Click allow on the "Allow position" popup.
  2. Click the "DROP MARKER" button to drop a marker.
  3. Drag the marker 500m from you.
  4. To try it again, press the "TRY AGAIN" button.

Tip - Zoom in or out to find the marker and for greater accuracy.
